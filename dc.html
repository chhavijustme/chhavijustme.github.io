District Online UX Designer District Online is an initiative by the Government of Assam, in association with Embedded Interaction Labs, to build an online e-governance portal. The project aims to build a seamless connectivity between citizens and administrative authorities.
Context Electronic Governance, is the use of Information and Communications Technology (ICT) to run or carry on the business of the Government of a Country. It ensures faster communication, transparent processing of grievances, accountability towards citizens and helps save cost of stationary and other related expenditure. My Responsibilities This is an ongoing project in the Embedded Interactions Lab, IIT Guwahati. The team is headed by Keyur Sorathia and comprises of two developers and me as a designer. The client, District Commissioner Office Kamrup, sends feedback and holds weekly meetings. User Personas To avoid “elastic” user needs in head, personas with a set of estimated characteristics were sketched and used for design. Task Flows The portal facilitates many processes which took months till now. Phase I included building it for application of leaves and public grievances. Leave applications are applied by employees and granted by the District Commissioner. There are total seven types of leaves mainly divided into earned and casual. Earlier, all this was done manually through paper leave forms and record books. The aim was to adopt it to the portal for quick addresal of leave requests and a systematic record.
